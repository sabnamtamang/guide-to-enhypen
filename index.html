About ENHYPEN

ENHYPEN is a seven-member boy group formed by Belift Lab under Hybe Corporation through the 2020 survival competition show I-Land.
The group consists of seven members: Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-ki. They debuted on November 30, 2020, with their first mini album Border: Day One.
