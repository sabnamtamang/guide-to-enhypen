Facts about Niki

  1. He’s from Okayama, Japan.
  2. He has two sister's(older and younger).
  3. Nicknames: Super Dancer, Little Michael Jackson.
  4. Ni-ki was in a group called SHINee Kids, where he was as Key through 2016-2017. He has danced with SHINee for multiple stages.
  5. He trained for eight months before taking part in I-LAND.
  6. He earned fourth place in the final of I-LAND (1,140,718 votes).
  7. He performed SuperM‘s Jopping in the first episode of I-LAND with two eliminated contestants, Hanbin and Nicholas.
  8. Ni-ki auditioned two times: Once in the 2018 BIGHIT GLOBAL AUDITION with K-POP COVER DANCE FESTIVAL with ‘Sayonara Hitori’ by Taemin of SHINee. He was 12 at the time. He re-auditioned in South Korea in 2019 with the cover ‘Lie’ by Jimin of BTS, and successfully passed the audition to join Belift Lab when he was 13.
  9. Ni-ki is also a well-known Shawol (SHINee fandom name) among Japanese fans. He was part of the SHINee KIDS when he was 11, and danced on stage with SHINee during their FIVE dome tour in Japan (Sept 24, 2017). Each of the five kids were ‘mini’ representations of the idols; Ni-ki was a ‘mini Key’ (Kim Kibum).
  10. Ni-ki can memorize whole new choreographies within 10-20 minutes, as confirmed both by him and by the members in I-Land. (I-Land: episode 2; episode 12). The members have also said Ni-ki learns choreographies the fastest.
  11. Ni-ki can play basic piano.
  12. Ni-ki has many hobbies including: dancing, photography, painting, soccer, bowling, fishing, gaming, and watching movies.
  13. He has been praised multiple times by the filming directors for his great and natural ability to pose in front of cameras.
  14. Home Alone is his favorite movie of all time, and Itaewon Class is his favorite Korean drama.
  15. He likes sleeping a lot. He said sleeping is what he does to relax.
  16. Ni-ki really loves bungeoppang. He also likes warmed up strawberries and salted watermelons. He dislikes pork.
  17. He enjoys photography. On music video sets, he will often borrow professional cameras from the staff to take pictures of the members.
  18. Ni-ki likes playing video games, especially FIFA which he used to play a lot with Heeseung and Sunghoon till late.
  19. Ni-ki, along with Sunoo, enjoy watching horror movies.
  20. Ni-ki is charismatic on stage and playful off stage. He is the maknae who loves to tease and play pranks on his hyungs, but he is also very warm and caring towards his members.
  21. He is a perfectionist when it comes to delivering performances. He pays close attention to the details of their performances, and will often stay until late at night to practice.
  22. Ni-ki is often known as the “4th Generation Dance Prodigy” and is regarded as one of the best dancers of his generation.
  23. Ni-ki contributed to the choreography of “Bite Me.”
  24. Ni-ki’s official nicknames (given by fans) are: ‘dance prodigy’, ‘legendary maknae’, ‘Nishimura-Manimura’, ‘Jjang-gu’ and ‘mandu’.
  25. His Korean nickname given by ENHYPEN members is “Nishimura Chulsoo".
  26. Ni-ki is ambidextrous. He can write with both hands.
