Facts about Sunghoon

  1. He has a sister who is five years younger.
  2. He was a former competitive figure skater who competed both nationally and internationally for 10 years. He passed all 8 Figure Skating Levels before retiring in early 2020.
  3. He has a dog named Gaeul (Autumn in English) and was born on July 8th, 2017.
  4. Education: Pangok High School
  5. Nicknames: Ice Prince (chosen by himself), Figure Skate Prince, ENHYPEN’s Handsome Member.
  6. He, Heeseung, Jay and Jungwon were trainees under Big Hit Entertainment.
  7. He trained for two years and one month before taking part in I-LAND.
  8. He ranked in sixth place in the final of I-LAND (1,088,413 votes).
  9. He and Jay performed together NCT U‘s The 7th Sense in the first episode of I-LAND.
  10. He won two silver medals in national ice skating competitions and represented South Korea in several international competitions.
  11. He also won the 2015 Asian Open Trophy and Lombardia Trophy as a novice skater.
  12. He debuted as a junior skater in the 2016-17 season. That time, he won a silver medal in the Men’s Junior Division at the 2016 Asian Open Trophy.
  13. His favorite color is white.
  14. His favorite season is autumn.
  15. His favorite ice-cream flavor is coffee.
  16. He became an official MC for Music Bank on October 8, 2021.
  17. Sunghoon is the brand ambassador for K-beauty brand Hince. He is the first member of the group to have a solo brand endorsement.
